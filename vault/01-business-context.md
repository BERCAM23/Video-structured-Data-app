- **Cousin's boss**: the buyer at Fox. Told the cousin that Fox can hire services from him so they keep the relationship while he studies. He reacted most strongly to the video search engine and data extraction idea.

## The Model

We operate as an AI agency / services provider to Fox. Bernardo and the cousin build it together. Fox is the first and anchor client.

## Why Fox Cares

Fox transmits content 24/7. Almost none of it exists as structured, queryable data. Converting every transmitted video into structured text data gives Fox:

- A context vault of everything they have aired
- A private AI that can answer questions about any moment of any broadcast
- Training data for future AI models
- Better reporting and strategy inputs

## Sales Sequence
